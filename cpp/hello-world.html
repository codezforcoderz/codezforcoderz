Life

Life revives you automatically if you hit the pipes. There will be 3 lives in each game session. After you have used all the 3 lives and hit a pipe, you will either die or you can use Diamond to revive again.





Shield

The shield instantly makes you intangible and protects you from death. It lasts for a short period of time. It appears on the sky after a certain time.





Diamond

Diamond is a currency in the game. Diamonds can be obtained just by playing the game. Play more, get more. It can be used to revive yourself when you're out of your Lives. It can be used 4 times in a game session. We have planned some new things like skins which can be purchased using diamonds in the future updates.
